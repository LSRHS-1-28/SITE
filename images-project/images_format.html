Image Formats

Images

I will be going over the following formats


  1. JPEG
  2. PNG
  3. GIF
  • A jpeg is a type of photo best used for more complex details. If you were to add shadow or a gradient effect this would be your best choice if you were to photoshop something
  • a png is a photo type that has different formats inside of it like png 8 or png 24. it is good for transparent photos and keeping the quality the same
  • gifs are tricky because they dont have many choices in terms of colors. gifs can also be used to create a moving image. or a "live" photo. it would not be a good format for photoshop
