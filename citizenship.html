What Can I Do To Become A Good Citizen

  • Participate In Class
  • Help Others When They Need Help
  • Show Your Teachers That You Appreciate Them And The Work They're Doing
  • Volunteer


    • Do You Belive That Honesty, Compassion, Respect, Responsibility and Courage Are Necessary Components Of Good Citizenship?

    I Do Believe That All 5 Of Those Are Necessary To Become A Good Citizen

    1. You Must Be Honest To Others And Yourself To Build Trust.
    2. You Must Be Compassionate To Show Others That We Are Equal And No One Has To Go Through Tough Times Alone
    3. You Must Show Respect Towards The Teachers Who Go The Extra Mile To See You Succeed
    4. You Must Be Responsible And Own Up To Your Actions If You Know Your In The Wrong
    5. Finally You Must Be Courageous To
    6. But there is still more to becoming a good citizen. You must learn to work with others and accept their differences. This is key as it will help you later in life to succeed

    Witnessing Citizenship

    I think one the most recent acts of good citizenship was with the recent hurricane in texas. People went out of there way to rescue strangers that were trapped in deadly flood waters. This is a great example of good citizenhip because they are going above and beyond the call of duty.
